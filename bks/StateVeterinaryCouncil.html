State Veterinary council shall consists of following 11 members
  • 4 Members – elected by registered veterinary practitioners.
  • 1 Members – Head of veterinary institution of state.
  • 3 Members – nominated by state government.
  • 1 Members – Director of veterinary services of the state.
  • 1 Members – State veterinary association.
  • 1 Members – Registrar of state veterinary council.
